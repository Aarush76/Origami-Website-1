Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • There are over 300 different species of pigeons that can be found throughout the world.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals, and have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • An adult panda spends as many as 14 hours a day eating and can eat 12 to 38 kilos of bamboo in a day.
  • Adult pandas can weigh as much as 45 kilos.
  • Newborn panda cubs are pink.

Intersting Facts about Camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped "Dromedary" camels and Two humped "Bactrian" camels.
  • Camels can sit comfortably in very hot sand due to pads of skin on their chest and knees.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
